Falaj contract interfaces

Select a contract to open its standalone testing interface.

  • IdentityRegistry
    Participant onboarding, roles, and KYC updates.
  • AEDStablecoin
    Mint, burn, pause, and transfer stablecoin flows.
  • BondToken
    Bond lifecycle actions, coupon metadata, and transfer checks.
  • DvPSettlement
    Create, fund, and execute delivery-versus-payment settlements.
  • FeeDistribution
    Collect and distribute validator/provider fees.
  • PaymentProcessor
    Deposit payments, manage exchange rates, and protocol fees.
  • RegulatedBridgeManager
    Manage bridge reserves, chains, and warp message handling.
  • ValidatorStakingManager
    Stake, register validators, and manage rewards.

Serve this folder over HTTP (for example: python3 -m http.server 8000).
